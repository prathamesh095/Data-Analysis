# Project Assumptions & Validation Notes

## Data Assumptions

- Customer IDs are unique and stable
- Order timestamps are reliable
- Activity data is representative of engagement
- Support tickets reflect customer friction

---

## Modeling Assumptions

- Churn defined via inactivity window
- RFM captures primary behavioral signals
- Purchase velocity approximates engagement momentum
- Rule-based scoring preferred for explainability

---

## Validation Checks Performed

- Segment distribution sanity check
- Revenue concentration by segment
- Outlier review for extreme customers
- Cohort retention trend validation

---

## Known Limitations

- No seasonality adjustment
- No causal modeling
- No marketing touchpoint attribution
- Refund behavior simplified

These are acceptable for an analytics-focused portfolio project.
